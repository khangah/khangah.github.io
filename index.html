Dorshe

Dorshe is an online market for supermarket purchases (Similar to Snap Market).

Supermarkets register in Dorshe, enter their products' information.

User choose a supermarket, add products to the shopping cart and Places an order. Supermarket receives the order and prepare it.

Delivery brings the order to destination and deliver it to the customer

Apps

  • Dorshe Market
    The main app, used by end user.

  • Dorshe Store Manager
    Used by supermarkets to manage products and orders.

  • Dorshe Delivery
    Used by delivery to manage sending orders

  • Dorshe Admin
    Used by Dorshe to manage all stores and settings
